User Story

Data managers at political advocacy organizations need to find the political districts for donor and volunteer lists. Without in-house GIS skill this gets outsourced at great expense or it just doesn’t get done at all. Our web-app will append all available political districts as columns to a user uploaded csv of addresses. Each row will be geocoded and then spatial queried to append the appropriate district values. After this processing, the file will be available for download. The data manager will then be able to create reports on their data and summarize accurately on their relevant political geographies.
